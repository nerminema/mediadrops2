2 Information Collected

We collect and use two types of information from you online: non-personal information, such as your device's operating system, which is automatically collected from every visitor, and personal information that you voluntarily provide or is automatically gathered during your interaction with us.

3 Uses of Information

We use non-personal information to manage and improve our online and mobile resources and to make informed business decisions about programs that may interest our customers. Personal information that you voluntarily provide is used to respond to your inquiries and deliver the services you request, along with other purposes detailed below. Rest assured, we do not sell or rent your personal information to third-party data vendors or marketing companies. However, we may disclose your information when legally required to do so.

4 Privacy Choices

You are not required to provide personal information to use most features of our online and mobile resources. Additionally, you can opt out of activities such as receiving newsletters or announcements by clicking the unsubscribe link in the email or through the online and mobile resources. If you wish to access, update, or delete the personal information we have collected about you, you can do so by reaching out to us using the contact details provided in this privacy statement.
